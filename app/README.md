How to Use
1. Launch the application on your Android device or emulator.
2. Sign up for a new account or log in using your registered email and password.
3. After successful login, you will be redirected to the **Home Screen**.
4. Browse available coffee products from the **Explore** section.
5. Select a coffee item to view details and add it to the **Cart**.
6. Open the **Cart** to review selected items.
7. Increase or decrease item quantity or remove items if needed.
8. Proceed to **Checkout** to place the order.
9. Complete the process and view the **Payment Success** screen.
10. Access **Orders**, **Wishlist**, **Profile**, and **Settings** from the menu.
